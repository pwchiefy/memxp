memxp Workflow Guide

1. EXPLORE: whats_saved() to see categories, services, totals
2. SEARCH: find(query) to find saved items by keyword
3. LOOK UP: recall(path) to retrieve a specific item
4. SAVE: remember(path, value, category, service, ...) to save something
5. HOW-TOS: read_instructions(name) to read saved procedures
6. SESSION: vault_session_start() for status snapshot (conflicts, alerts)

Path convention: <service>/<resource>/<detail>
  Examples: aws/s3/access_key, postgres/prod/url, gitea/admin/password

Guide convention: <service>-<action>
  Examples: vps-deploy, caddy-config, postgres-backup
